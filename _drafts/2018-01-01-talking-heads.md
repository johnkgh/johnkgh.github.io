I have a lot of derisive things to say about personal blogs. As a literary form, they lack substance and are largely inconsequential, written usually by the most smug and insufferable personalities who are deluded into believing that what they have to say is original. That they have some astute point of view of the world that neither you nor I have ever considered before. That they are capable of experiencing life and existence at a higher level of consciousness than you and I, and have generously devoted their time and a couple hundred words to unlock your potential, open your eyes, and clean your life up.
