This post is going to be an incomplete compilation of things that had some kind of an impact on me.
I really wish I could have started documenting this from an early of an age as possible, to see how my interests have changed and evolved over time.
The line drawn for what makes this list is arbitrary.
I'd like to think everything here is of profound and substantive impact for me, and I'll refrain from adding things willy nilly.
I'll most likely sleep on these things before I add them to the list.
I'll try to add explanations as to why I like these things to reduce some of the false positives.
It's likely that some things might slip through the cracks though.

They will mostly be pulled from works of music or literature.
I can't say that I've ever been moved or deeply affected by a painting, equation, program, etc.
That's not to say it won't pop up, I just haven't had the opportunity just hasn't come up yet.
The Barry's paradox is quite interesting, but it doesn't quite make the list.
