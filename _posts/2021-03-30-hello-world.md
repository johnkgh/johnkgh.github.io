Welcome!

I figure since everyone and their mother has a blog, I should start one as well.
While most people in the computing industry usually only write about technical subjects in their own blogs, I'll be discussing pretty much anything that piques my interest.
Though I haven't written prolifically for my own sake for the majority of my life, I've always had plenty of topics on my mind that I could talk interminably about.
However, I'm acutely aware that the things posted on this site are not and will not be great and profound works of art, so there will be no pretense that I'm producing material of such caliber.
I'll emphasize that this is just a personal experiment for me to improve my own writing.
After all, I believe self-expression of any sort is crucial for us to exist as human beings - especially in today's era of cliched nihilism.

<!-- more -->

Whatever my level of writing was prior to starting this blog, it has likely worsened up to this point due to creative and linguistic atrophy.
Hopefully, this experiment can reverse some of those effects.
Regardless of whether or not I'm a "good writer", it seems as if this is all that I have right now, given my lack of skill in other expressive pursuits like music or art.
Jotting down what I'm thinking about and what I feel seems to be the next best avenue for me to communicate some part of my mere meaningless existence to myself and the reader.
Should you decide to stay, I sincerely thank you for being able to share my thoughts with you; I hope you find something interesting here and enjoy what you read.
