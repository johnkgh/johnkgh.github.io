There is nothing notable about Ruby Mountain in the North Cascades, but its position to marvel at the other mountains in the vicinity is much appreciated.
Although we missed the powder window in favor of mashed potatoes, I imagine the skiing would be nice as well, so I definitely need to come back early on a cold bluebird day!
