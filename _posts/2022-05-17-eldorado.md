Eldorado Peak is a [50 Classic Ski Descent of North America](https://www.50classicskidescents.com/project-4/qc6gu4t6kzheswrg4qzfd3s0td989c) that shouldn't be.
The quality of skiing itself was mediocre but the views were _incredible_.
We thought we were gonna get whited-out but pressed on instead.
I'm happy we did because we were rewarded with stunning views near the summit that faded as quickly as it opened up.
