Mt. Rainier is just an absolute unit looming over the Seattle skyline from afar.
A 14400' ruler from sea level if you were curious what that might look like.
Unfortunately, it turns out the views from the peak just aren't that good - definitely a mountain to awe at than look from the top of.
We skied off the summit down to Nisqually Bridge, an unforgettable 10500' continuous ski descent.
