Kind of a throwaway post.
Trying to eek out the last bit of fun on the volcanos as everything that I wanted to ski in Washington got decimated by the heat.
I don't think these outings were anything special and I'm not one to boast about stats, but I got to experience just a _taste_ of decent cardio.
Up Shasta in 4:00, up Hood in 2:40, almost 20k of vert on Adams over two days.
I know most of this was probably due to living at elevation while in the Sierra, but it planted the idea that I can maybe achieve this level of fitness regularly if I really get my stuff together.
