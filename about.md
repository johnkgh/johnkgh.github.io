It's always interesting to hear how people measure the passage of time in their life.
For me, I've used movies, music, my grade/year in school, jobs, etc, to mark the various eras of my life.
These days, I find myself measuring time by how I spend my time outdoors.
This site is a way for me to note those standout days, classic outings, or otherwise memorable experiences.
I also want a way of storing some of my favorite pictures and not forgetting about them in a hard drive forever.
That being said, a lot of these posts were written way after the outing took place so this is definitely an active work in progress!
