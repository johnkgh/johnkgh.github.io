It's always interesting to hear how people measure the passage of time in their life.
For me, I've previously used movies, music, my grade-year in school, jobs, etc, to mark the various eras of my life.
These days, I find myself measuring time by how I spend my time outdoors.
This site is a way for me to note those standout days, classic outings, or otherwise memorable experiences.

I'm also starting this site because I think I occasionally take good pictures (albeit with a humble iPhone camera), and want to store them somewhere without them being stuck in a hard drive forever.
I just don't think Instagram is adequate for my love of landscape photos and the sheer number of them that I take.
That being said, a lot of these posts were written way after the outing took place (like years) - this is definitely a work in progress!
